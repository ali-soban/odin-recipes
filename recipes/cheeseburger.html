Cheese Burger Recipe

Description

Blue cheese burgers aren't just basic hamburgers — they're a real treat, and the wise cook will make up a dozen or so for the freezer. If you like blue cheese, you'll never forget these burgers.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced fresh chives
  • 4 ounces blue cheese, crumbled
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide ground beef into equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the burger patties on the grill or pan. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare to medium doneness, adjusting the time based on your preference.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the buns and adding your desired toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, and mustard.
