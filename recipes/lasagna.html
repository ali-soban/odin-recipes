Lasagna Recipe

Description

It is rectangular shaped, with layers including its toppings, cheese, noodles and much more. These also vary depending on local traditions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the meat mixture. Simmer for about 10 minutes, then remove from heat.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: In a baking dish, layer the ingredients in the following order: a thin layer of meat sauce, lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, shredded mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of shredded mozzarella cheese.
